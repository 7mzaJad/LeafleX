Our Services

LeafleX provides an innovative digital compliance service that helps pharmaceutical companies transition from traditional paper-based product leaflets to machine-readable digital formats (XML) compliant with HL7 FHIR standards and local regulatory requirements. Designed for small-to-mid manufacturers, it offers an affordable, user-friendly solution that automates data conversion, ensures accuracy with built-in validation, and reduces operational inefficiencies. By streamlining workflows and supporting digital transformation, LeafleX empowers companies to meet regulatory demands effortlessly and cost-effectively.

Why Choose Leaflex?

Efficiency: Automates manual processes, reducing errors and saving time.

Compliance: Built with regulatory requirements in mind for effortless auditing.

Ease of Use: An intuitive interface designed for teams of all technical backgrounds.
